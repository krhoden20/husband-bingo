The Setup
StepWhat You Do
01Each player fills in 4 predictions per husband — 12 predictions total on your card (3 columns × 4 rows).
02Predictions are about what Jason, Jordan, or Eric will actually do tonight. Be specific. Be petty. Be right.
03Once you're done, tap Lock Card. You can't edit after locking.
04Game goes live when all 3 cards are locked.
